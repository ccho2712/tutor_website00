Privacy Policy

Effective: November 04, 2025

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how Tutor5** collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. Data We Collect
Type Examples
Personal Name, email, phone, school, parent name
Academic Subjects, grades, progress, quiz scores
Technical IP, browser, device, login times
Usage Courses viewed, time spent, tutor ratings
2. How We Use Data
  • Match you with the best tutor
  • Send progress reports to parents
  • Improve platform with analytics
  • Send exam tips and reminders
3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. Shared only with:

  • Tutors: Name, subject, session time
  • Parents: Child’s progress (if linked)
  • Payment Partners: Encrypted card info
  • Legal: If required by law
4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for login, preferences, and analytics. Disable in browser, but some features may stop.

5. Your Rights
  • View, download, or delete your data
  • Opt out of emails anytime
  • Request data portability

Email: privacy@tutor5star.com

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Users under 13 require verified parental consent. We delete unverified accounts.

7. Data Security

SSL encryption · Regular audits · Limited staff access
